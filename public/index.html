How to read your arena
🏰Each town is a project. It grows from your real work — Outpost → Hamlet → Walled Town → Citadel → Capital.
🚶Each worker is a session. Named, and the busy ones become veterans (★).
🛸Each drone is a subagent, tethered to the worker that launched it.
🏘️Subfolders are districts — satellite hamlets ringing the capital.
🔨Tools build the town: Bash→forge · Edit→workshop · Read→watchtower · Web→war-gate · Task→barracks.
🛣️Roads wear in between projects you work in the same sitting.
⟿Emissaries dart between towns when one project touches another's files — a scout reads, a builder edits, a runner runs.
🌙It's alive: day/night follows your clock, quiet towns dim, and finishing a turn makes a town glow.
